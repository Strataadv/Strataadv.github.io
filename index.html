Common questions

What does Keldron actually identify?
Keldron helps identify the bottleneck slowing business performance across areas like operations, inventory, finance, and sales. It also highlights likely root causes, risk factors, KPI priorities, and first-priority actions.
Who gets the most value from Keldron?
Owner-led businesses that know something is underperforming but do not yet know what to fix first. If margins feel tight, operations feel heavy, or growth feels slower than expected, Keldron is built for you.
How fast do I get the report?
Usually within minutes. You answer a short diagnostic and Keldron generates a structured report you can read immediately — including key findings, risks, KPIs, quick wins, and a 90-day action plan.
Is this accounting software or a dashboard?
No. Keldron is not bookkeeping software or another dashboard. It is a self-serve business diagnostic that helps clarify the issue most likely slowing performance and what to do next.
Do I need to upload sensitive financial data?
No. Keldron works from top-line business context — the kind of things you already know about your business. No spreadsheets, no sensitive numbers, no integrations required.
What is included in the report?
A typical report includes an executive snapshot, key findings, root causes, strategic recommendations, quick wins, KPI framework, risks and mitigations, and a 90-day action plan.
